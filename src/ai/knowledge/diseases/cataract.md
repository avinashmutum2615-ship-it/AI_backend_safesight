# Cataract

A cataract is the clouding of the natural lens of the eye.

## Symptoms

- Blurred vision
- Glare from lights
- Difficulty seeing at night
- Faded colors

## Diagnosis

A cataract is diagnosed using a slit lamp examination and visual acuity test.

## Treatment

The definitive treatment is cataract surgery (phacoemulsification) with intraocular lens (IOL) implantation.

## Prevention

- Wear UV-protective sunglasses
- Control diabetes
- Avoid smoking
- Regular eye examinations
